## Overview

Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021, primarily known for building and studying the Claude family of large language models. It publishes foundational mechanistic interpretability research via transformer-circuits.pub, and frames AI safety as a core research priority alongside capability development.

## Key Facts

- Publishes interpretability research at transformer-circuits.pub — [[emotion-concepts-llm]]
- Developed Claude Sonnet 4.5, the model studied in the emotions paper — [[emotion-concepts-llm]]
- Interpretability team extracted emotion vectors from Claude Sonnet 4.5 activations — [[emotion-concepts-llm]]
- Advocates for monitoring internal model states (emotion vectors, concept vectors) as alignment tools — [[emotion-concepts-llm]]

## Role in Wiki

Anthropic is the primary research organization in this wiki's ML/interpretability thread. Their transformer-circuits research program (mechanistic interpretability) is the methodological foundation for understanding how LLMs represent concepts internally. Their work directly connects the interpretability thread to the AI safety thread.
